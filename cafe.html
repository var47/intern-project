ABOUT RIDER'S PIT

Rider's pit is a motorcycle themed cafe which was built for the Riders by the Riders. Here you can have your shot of caffeine to boost your adrenaline in your favourite coffee with your riding buddies. And don't you feel lonely if you don' have any friend who is a petrolhead like you, because at Rider's Pit, you will find a lot of like minded petrolheads.

In addition to our open minded petrolhead frienfly space, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.

"We all need fuel for ourselves in the form of coffee or tea just like our bikes need petrol."

Head Chef, Coffeeist and another petrolhead: Kumar Vivek

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 5pm.

Address: Green Heritage, Boring Road, Patna
